how to play

objective

Use the bat to bounce a ball and chip away at rows of bricks.

controls

Move the bat with the LEFT/RIGHT arrow keys or use the mouse. Hit SPACEBAR or click the game screen to play.

to win

Destroy all bricks in the level.

If you drop the ball, you'll lose a life. Drop the ball with zero lives left and the game is over!
